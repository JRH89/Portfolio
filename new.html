Made in Unreal Engine 4. An action-adventure game featuring a scarecrow who has magically come to life as the main character. You befriend a fox and crow who follow along and aid you in your adventures. The fox can be used to access hidden areas and the crow can be utilized to get a bird's eye view of enemy territory or get a fresh perspective on how to progress further. Explore caves, save the day, and learn what it means to be alive in this thrilling and sentimental joyride that will have you remembering what the human experience is really about...connections and relationships with those around us.
