Adventures In Fall Hollow


Using a voxel plugin I am creating a cave system that will connect the entire map on a subterranean level and provide another dimension of exploration with obstacles to overcome and puzzles to solve.

The main character is followed by his fox and crow companion who can be possessed at any time. Each character has unique abilities that aid in solving puzzles, defeating enemies, etc.

All of the fauna in Fall Hollow have independant eat/sleep schedules and will flee from danger when approached.

Dynamic weather with random rain and snow storms. The birds stop singing and fog gets thick, the lighting dims, and the storm begins.

This fireball attack has excellent smoke trails and damage effectiveness. Each enemy drops a random piece of loot when defeated.
